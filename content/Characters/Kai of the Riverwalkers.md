Kai of the Riverwalkers is a [[Wanderborne]] [[Drakona]] Warden of the Elements [[Druid]]. He has spent considerable time with the nomadic Riverwalker tribe and upon learning a secret truth reserved for the Leadership of his people, Kai has decided to leave his tribe and seek to mend the divisions that plague the continent... and himself.

On his way to Haven, he passed through [[Convergence]] where he met with [[Felorian Clankmarius]]. Upon listening to his pleas and sensing his very raw emotions, Kai decided he was telling the truth about the Transference ritual and that he would begin to put his abilities to good work by helping Felorian rediscover himself. The two have set out for Crystal Bridge.
