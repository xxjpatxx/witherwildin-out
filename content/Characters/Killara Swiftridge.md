Killara Swiftridge is a [[Ridgeborne]] [[Halfling]] [[Wayfinder]] [[Ranger]]. She is primarily seeking answers about her sister, [[Raewyn Ridgefoot|Raewyn]]'s, disappearance. She was last seen near the [[Divine Perch]] some time around the same time as the partial blinding of the Great Owl Nikta. She is friendly with [[Drakhar Greenthorn]], though she doesn't know him as well as her sister did. She had also previously come into contact with the human wizard [[Felorian Clankmarius]] in Crystal Bridge. He took a special interest in her and warned her about Haven's ambitions in the region.

Killara is a lithe halfling that travels the wilds and mountains of Fanewick and Haven in tactical clothing that aims to keep her traversal swift. She is naturally at home in the mountains and rocky terrain. Her number one concern is uncovering the truth about her sister's disappearance.
