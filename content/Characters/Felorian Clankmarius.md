Felorian Clankmarius is a [[Loreborne]] [[Clank]] School of Knowledge [[Wizard]]. He was previously a very powerful human wizard who, in order to seek knowledge, conducted an Ritual of Arcane Conscious Transference, imbuing a Clank body with this mind and memories. The spell succeeded, somewhat, though Felorian now only remembers fragments of his past life and consciously realizes but a shard of his latent magical potential.

Felorian has now escaped Haven City, and met [[Kai of the Riverwalkers]] in [[Convergence]]. His best chance to regain access to the Magus Society rests with his contacts in [[Crystal Bridge]].
