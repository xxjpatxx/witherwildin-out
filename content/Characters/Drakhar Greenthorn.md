Drakhar Greenthorn is a [[Wildborne]] [[Simiah]]-[[Giant]] Call of the Slayer [[Warrior]]. He primarily acts as a guide for individuals looking to relocate from [[Haven]] to [[Fanewick]] or vice-versa. He is an experienced navigator of the [[Tangled Coast]]. He is friendly with [[Killara Swiftridge]] though he has had more interactions with her elder sister, [[Raewyn Swiftridge]]. His primary concern is securing a cure for a mysterious wasting illness that has found root in his tribe.

Drakhar is a large, lumbering humanoid with features primarily reminiscent of a silverback gorilla. He wields a heavy two-handed warhammer and is adorned in chainmail armor with leafy green accents. A leather belt with gold buckle is cinched around his waist.
